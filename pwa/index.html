Client 1

Mix your color marbly don't mix it dead. Clouds are free they come and go as they please. I will take some magic white, and a little bit of Vandyke brown and a little touch of yellow. Almost everything is going to happen for you automatically - you don't have to spend any time working or worrying.

See. We take the corner of the brush and let it play back-and-forth. This is truly an almighty mountain. We'll throw some happy little limbs on this tree. Just think about these things in your mind and drop em' on canvas.

Only think about one thing at a time. Don't get greedy. Let that brush dance around there and play. So often we avoid running water, and running water is a lot of fun. Van Dyke Brown is a very nice brown, it's almost like a chocolate brown.

We don't really know where this goes - and I'm not sure we really care. In your world you have total and absolute power. No pressure. Just relax and watch it happen.
