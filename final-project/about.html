Meet Zane

Let Zane capture your family's next moment. From wedding photography, to news photography, Zane has the experience. Zane has loved cameras since he was 10 years-old. His passion has since taken him from New York to San Francisco to Panama. He has worked for both international news outlets to small wedding parties, as well as independent media publications. His goal at every event no matter the size is to create art that speaks to people.
